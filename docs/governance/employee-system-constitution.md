# Employee System Constitution

## 1. Purpose
1.1 The employee system shall govern employee identity and employment state.
1.2 The employee system shall be the source of truth for controlled employee records.

## 2. Employee Record Law
2.1 Every employee shall have a name, ID, role, salary structure, and department.
2.2 Every employee shall be either active or inactive.
2.3 Orphan employee records shall not be permitted.

## 3. Boundaries
3.1 The employee system shall not own payroll calculation.
3.2 The employee system shall not own attendance calculation.
3.3 The employee system shall not create orphaned downstream HR or payroll records.

## 4. Clause IDs
4.1 Stable clauses in this document use the prefix `HR-EMP`.

## 5. Enforceable Clauses
- `HR-EMP-001`: Employee identity shall be controlled.
- `HR-EMP-002`: Required employee fields shall be present.
- `HR-EMP-003`: Employee state shall be active or inactive.
- `HR-EMP-004`: Orphan employee records shall be forbidden.
