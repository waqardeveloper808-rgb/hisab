# Inventory-Accounting Integration

Updated: 2026-04-23
Status: Governing stock-to-accounting linkage law.

## 1. Integration Authority

### 1.1 Clause IDs

- `INV-ACC-001`: Inventory movement shall produce accounting impact where governed by stock and valuation rules.
- `INV-ACC-002`: Stock movement and accounting impact shall not diverge.
- `INV-ACC-003`: Inventory accounting linkage shall remain auditable and traceable.

### 1.2 Account linkage

- `INV-ACC-010`: Stock asset account shall represent stock truth.
- `INV-ACC-011`: COGS account shall receive sale-linked cost impact.
- `INV-ACC-012`: Purchase account or inventory asset treatment shall follow governed posting logic.

### 1.3 Integrity law

- `INV-ACC-020`: No mismatch between stock and accounting shall be accepted as valid.
- `INV-ACC-021`: Inventory movement shall equal accounting impact where the governing rule requires posting.
- `INV-ACC-022`: Mapping gaps shall block governing inventory-accounting completion.
