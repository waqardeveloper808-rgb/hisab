# Preview Engine Constitution

Updated: 2026-04-23
Status: Governing preview truth law.

## 1. Preview Authority

### 1.1 Clause IDs

- `PRV-001`: Preview shall be identical to final PDF for the same governed document state.
- `PRV-002`: Preview shall not contain debug content.
- `PRV-003`: Preview shall not duplicate sections or invent extra layout.

### 1.2 Truth rules

- `PRV-010`: Preview shall represent the final state exactly.
- `PRV-011`: Preview shall not drift from PDF layout, content, or visibility rules.
- `PRV-012`: Any preview divergence shall be treated as a governance defect.

### 1.3 Forbidden behavior

- `PRV-020`: Preview shall not be a lower-fidelity substitute for the PDF.
- `PRV-021`: Preview shall not render stale template logic.
- `PRV-022`: Preview shall not expose hidden helper content that is absent from PDF truth.
