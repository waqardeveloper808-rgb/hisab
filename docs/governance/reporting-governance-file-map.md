## 2. Retain
2.1 Higher-level governance packs remain in place and are retained as upstream authority.
2.2 Runtime reporting, reconciliation, and intelligence implementation files are retained as implementation-only.

## 3. Replace
3.1 Legacy reporting assumptions shall be replaced by the new constitutional pack.
3.2 Any reporting guidance that permits independent report truth shall be replaced.

## 4. Deprecate
4.1 Free-form report calculations shall be deprecated as governance truth.
4.2 Hidden mismatch acceptance shall be deprecated.
4.3 UI-derived reporting truth shall be deprecated.

## 5. Ignore as Source of Truth
5.1 `.next/` shall be treated as build output.
5.2 Generated artifacts shall not be treated as source of truth.

## 6. Inheritance
6.1 Future prompts shall inherit from the governing constitution hierarchy in `docs/governance`.
6.2 Future control points and audits shall reference stable clause IDs.
