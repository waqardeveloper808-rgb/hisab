# HR Audit Protocol Seeds

## 1. Scope
1.1 These seeds define audit expectations for HR, attendance, payroll, and accounting integration.

## 2. Audit Expectations
2.1 Payroll versus accounting validation shall be mandatory.
2.2 Attendance versus payroll validation shall be mandatory.
2.3 Anomaly detection shall be mandatory.
2.4 Evidence shall be captured for each nonconformity.

## 3. Root-Cause Zones
3.1 Payroll engine.
3.2 Attendance layer.
3.3 Employee data layer.
3.4 Accounting mapping layer.

## 4. Clause IDs
4.1 Stable clauses in this document use the prefix `HR-AUD`.

## 5. Enforceable Clauses
- `HR-AUD-001`: Audit shall compare payroll and accounting.
- `HR-AUD-002`: Audit shall compare attendance and payroll.
- `HR-AUD-003`: Audit shall detect anomalies.
- `HR-AUD-004`: Audit shall retain evidence.
- `HR-AUD-005`: Audit shall classify root-cause zones.
