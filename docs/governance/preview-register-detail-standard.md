# Preview, Register, and Detail Standard

Updated: 2026-04-23
Status: Mandatory record-viewing law for workspace workflows.

## 1. Record Journey Law

### 1.1 Clause IDs

- `UX-PREVIEW-001`: When a register opens, the user shall be able to inspect records without workflow confusion.
- `UX-PREVIEW-002`: Clicking a record row shall open a preview or detail experience that exceeds mid-market accounting SaaS expectations.
- `UX-PREVIEW-003`: Preview shall never be a dead end.

### 1.2 Pattern law

- `UX-PREVIEW-010`: Preview and detail patterns shall be explicitly governed.
- `UX-PREVIEW-011`: The permitted patterns are slide panel, side preview, split-view, and full detail page.
- `UX-PREVIEW-012`: The chosen pattern shall be intentional and documented for the route family.

### 1.3 Continuity law

- `UX-PREVIEW-020`: Preview, full detail, and edit flow shall remain consistent.
- `UX-PREVIEW-021`: The user shall be able to return, edit, print, download, send, or continue workflow safely.
- `UX-PREVIEW-022`: Preview state shall preserve the original record context and route identity.
