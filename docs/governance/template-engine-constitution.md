# Template Engine Constitution

Updated: 2026-04-23
Status: Governing layout-definition law for document templates.

## 1. Template Authority

### 1.1 Clause IDs

- `TMP-001`: Templates shall be structured layout definitions, not free design surfaces.
- `TMP-002`: Templates shall use allowed governed blocks only.
- `TMP-003`: Templates shall not grant arbitrary HTML freedom where document truth can be broken.

### 1.2 Allowed blocks

- `TMP-010`: Allowed blocks shall include header, invoice information box, delivery information box, customer information, items table, totals section, and footer.
- `TMP-011`: Block positioning shall remain governed and measurable.
- `TMP-012`: Spacing, alignment, and grouping shall remain constrained by law, not by uncontrolled editor freedom.

### 1.3 Forbidden behavior

- `TMP-020`: A template shall not duplicate governed sections without explicit rule support.
- `TMP-021`: A template shall not hide layout violations behind styling freedom.
- `TMP-022`: A template shall not redefine business semantics.
