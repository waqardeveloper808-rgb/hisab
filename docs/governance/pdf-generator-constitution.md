# PDF Generator Constitution

Updated: 2026-04-23
Status: Governing PDF output law.

## 1. PDF Authority

### 1.1 Clause IDs

- `PDF-001`: PDF shall be the authoritative document output.
- `PDF-002`: PDF formatting shall follow governed layout and numeric rules.
- `PDF-003`: PDF shall not introduce random footer text.

### 1.2 Formatting law

- `PDF-010`: Numeric values shall use governed comma formatting and stable decimal precision.
- `PDF-011`: Text shall not touch borders; padding shall be preserved from governed layout rules.
- `PDF-012`: Overflow shall be prevented or explicitly constrained by the template and renderer contract.

### 1.3 Compliance features

- `PDF-020`: PDF shall support bilingual output where the document contract requires it.
- `PDF-021`: Page numbering shall be governed and consistent.
- `PDF-022`: Compliance marks, QR content, and required metadata shall render only when governed by compliance law.
