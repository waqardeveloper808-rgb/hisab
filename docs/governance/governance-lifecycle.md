# Governance Lifecycle

## 1. Lifecycle Stages
1.1 Create.
1.2 Approve.
1.3 Adopt.
1.4 Enforce.
1.5 Audit.
1.6 Correct.
1.7 Retest.
1.8 Close.
1.9 Revise.
1.10 Deprecate.

## 2. Change Authority
2.1 Governance may be changed only through explicit governed revisions.
2.2 Clause revisions shall be tracked with stable identifiers and version context.
2.3 Deprecated clauses shall remain traceable.
2.4 Module laws shall be versioned.
2.5 Registry files shall be updated after governance changes.

## 3. Clause IDs
3.1 Stable clauses in this document use the prefix `SYS-LIFE`.

## 4. Enforceable Clauses
- `SYS-LIFE-001`: Governance shall follow the defined lifecycle.
- `SYS-LIFE-002`: Clause revisions shall be tracked.
- `SYS-LIFE-003`: Deprecated clauses shall remain traceable.
- `SYS-LIFE-004`: Registry files shall be updated after governance changes.
