# Reporting Audit Protocol Seeds

## 1. Scope
1.1 These seeds define audit expectations for reporting, reconciliation, and cross-module consistency.

## 2. Audit Expectations
2.1 Expected versus actual comparison shall be mandatory.
2.2 Cross-module validation shall be mandatory.
2.3 Anomaly detection shall be mandatory.
2.4 Evidence shall be captured for each nonconformity.

## 3. Root-Cause Zones
3.1 Reporting engine.
3.2 Mapping layer.
3.3 Accounting engine.
3.4 Inventory engine.
3.5 VAT engine.

## 4. Clause IDs
4.1 Stable clauses in this document use the prefix `RPT-AUD`.

## 5. Enforceable Clauses
- `RPT-AUD-001`: Audit shall compare expected and actual state.
- `RPT-AUD-002`: Audit shall validate cross-module consistency.
- `RPT-AUD-003`: Audit shall detect anomalies.
- `RPT-AUD-004`: Audit shall retain evidence.
- `RPT-AUD-005`: Audit shall classify root-cause zones.
