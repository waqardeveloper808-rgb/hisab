# Inventory Engine Constitution

Updated: 2026-04-23
Status: Governing inventory truth, quantity, and value law.

## 1. Authority

### 1.1 Clause IDs

- `INV-CON-001`: Inventory shall be a controlled quantity and value system.
- `INV-CON-002`: The inventory engine shall own stock truth.
- `INV-CON-003`: The inventory engine shall not accept UI-based stock manipulation.

### 1.2 Boundaries

- `INV-CON-010`: Inventory truth shall change only through governed movement logic.
- `INV-CON-011`: Silent stock changes shall not be allowed.
- `INV-CON-012`: All stock changes shall pass through the movement engine.

### 1.3 Forbidden behavior

- `INV-CON-020`: No manual stock editing shall be accepted as governing truth.
- `INV-CON-021`: No stock change without movement shall be accepted.
- `INV-CON-022`: No hidden adjustment shall be treated as valid inventory truth.
