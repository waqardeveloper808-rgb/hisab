# Retest and Closure Protocol

## 1. Retest Preconditions
1.1 The corrective action shall have an exact target.
1.2 The retest shall reference the source audit.
1.3 The retest shall use evidence relevant to the failure.

## 2. Retest Outcomes
2.1 Pass.
2.2 Fail.
2.3 Reopen.

## 3. Closure Criteria
3.1 No closure shall occur without evidence.
3.2 No closure shall occur if related control points still fail.
3.3 Reopened items shall preserve prior audit history.

## 4. Permanent Record Requirements
4.1 Audit history shall remain auditable after closure.
4.2 Retest evidence shall remain linked to the original audit trail.

## 5. Clause IDs
5.1 Stable clauses in this document use the prefix `AUD-RET`.

## 6. Enforceable Clauses
- `AUD-RET-001`: Retest shall have preconditions.
- `AUD-RET-002`: Retest shall produce pass, fail, or reopen outcome.
- `AUD-RET-003`: No closure shall occur without evidence.
- `AUD-RET-004`: Related failing control points shall block closure.
- `AUD-RET-005`: Reopened items shall preserve audit history.
