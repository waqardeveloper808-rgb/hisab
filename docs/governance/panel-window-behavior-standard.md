# Panel, Window, and Card Behavior Standard

Updated: 2026-04-23
Status: Mandatory panel behavior law for adjustable workspace surfaces.

## 1. Panel Law

### 1.1 Clause IDs

- `UX-PANEL-001`: Business panels and windows shall be user-adjustable where appropriate.
- `UX-PANEL-002`: Declared adjustable surfaces shall support collapse, expand, close, and restore.
- `UX-PANEL-003`: Resize shall be supported where appropriate to the declared panel type.

### 1.2 Workflow continuity

- `UX-PANEL-010`: Panel state shall not corrupt workflow state.
- `UX-PANEL-011`: Temporary overlay patterns shall not trap the user.
- `UX-PANEL-012`: Close and collapse behavior shall be intentional and reversible.

### 1.3 Productivity law

- `UX-PANEL-020`: Registers and previews shall support split-view patterns for high-productivity use.
- `UX-PANEL-021`: Card and window interactions shall feel modern, premium, and enterprise-grade.
- `UX-PANEL-022`: Interactive surfaces shall preserve user context when collapsed or reopened.
