# Timesheet and Attendance Constitution

## 1. Purpose
1.1 The timesheet system shall govern attendance, hours, overtime, and absence.
1.2 Attendance shall be a required basis for payroll processing.

## 2. Timesheet Law
2.1 Work hours shall be tracked by period.
2.2 Overtime shall be tracked explicitly.
2.3 Absence shall be tracked explicitly.
2.4 Timesheets shall be editable only with auditability.

## 3. Boundaries
3.1 No payroll shall be processed without attendance basis.
3.2 Hidden attendance mutation shall be forbidden.
3.3 Attendance changes shall retain who changed what and when.

## 4. Clause IDs
4.1 Stable clauses in this document use the prefix `HR-TMS`.

## 5. Enforceable Clauses
- `HR-TMS-001`: Attendance shall be tracked.
- `HR-TMS-002`: Overtime shall be tracked.
- `HR-TMS-003`: Absence shall be tracked.
- `HR-TMS-004`: Payroll shall require attendance basis.
- `HR-TMS-005`: Attendance edits shall be auditable.
