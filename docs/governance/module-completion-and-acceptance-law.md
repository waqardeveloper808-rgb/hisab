# Module Completion and Acceptance Law

## 1. Module States
1.1 Not started.
1.2 Partial.
1.3 Conditionally compliant.
1.4 Compliant.
1.5 Failed.
1.6 Reopened.
1.7 Deprecated.

## 2. Acceptance Requirements
2.1 Constitutional coverage shall exist.
2.2 Control-point mapping shall exist.
2.3 Audit coverage shall exist.
2.4 Evidence shall be available.
2.5 No unresolved critical failure shall remain.
2.6 Retest success shall exist where needed.

## 3. Rejection Rules
3.1 UI existence shall not equal completion.
3.2 Route existence shall not equal completion.
3.3 Partial working flow shall not equal completion.
3.4 Preview-mode success shall not equal production readiness.

## 4. Clause IDs
4.1 Stable clauses in this document use the prefix `SYS-MOD`.

## 5. Enforceable Clauses
- `SYS-MOD-001`: Module state shall be explicit.
- `SYS-MOD-002`: Acceptance shall require governance coverage and evidence.
- `SYS-MOD-003`: UI existence shall not equal completion.
- `SYS-MOD-004`: Preview success shall not equal production readiness.
