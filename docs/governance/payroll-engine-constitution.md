# Payroll Engine Constitution

## 1. Purpose
1.1 The payroll engine shall calculate pay for defined periods under governed rules.
1.2 The payroll engine shall produce payslips and payroll output records.

## 2. Salary Structure
2.1 Payroll shall support basic salary, allowances, and deductions.
2.2 Payroll shall process by defined payroll period.
2.3 Payroll shall generate payslips as a governed output.

## 3. Boundaries
3.1 Payroll shall not operate as a standalone truth layer.
3.2 Payroll shall not bypass attendance basis.
3.3 Manual salary override shall require logs and audit visibility.
3.4 Orphan payroll records shall not be permitted.

## 4. Clause IDs
4.1 Stable clauses in this document use the prefix `HR-PAY`.

## 5. Enforceable Clauses
- `HR-PAY-001`: Payroll shall be period-based.
- `HR-PAY-002`: Payroll shall support governed salary structure.
- `HR-PAY-003`: Payroll shall generate payslips.
- `HR-PAY-004`: Manual salary override shall be logged.
- `HR-PAY-005`: Orphan payroll records shall be forbidden.
