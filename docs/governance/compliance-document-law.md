# Compliance Document Law

Updated: 2026-04-23
Status: Governing ZATCA and future EU e-invoicing compliance law.

## 1. Compliance Authority

### 1.1 Clause IDs

- `CMP-001`: Compliance validation shall be required before finalization.
- `CMP-002`: ZATCA requirements shall be enforced at design level.
- `CMP-003`: Future EU e-invoicing compatibility shall remain an explicit design requirement.

### 1.2 ZATCA rules

- `CMP-010`: QR code placement shall be governed and deterministic.
- `CMP-011`: VAT logic shall remain consistent with the document contract and compliance state.
- `CMP-012`: Required metadata fields shall be validated before finalization.
- `CMP-013`: Compliance failure shall block finalization.

### 1.3 EU extensibility

- `CMP-020`: The document system shall remain extensible for EU e-invoicing rules.
- `CMP-021`: EU semantic mapping, transport, and metadata rules shall be supportable without redesigning the document constitution.
- `CMP-022`: Compliance law shall permit country-specific overlays without breaking document truth.
