# Stock Valuation Constitution

Updated: 2026-04-23
Status: Governing stock valuation and cost-layer law.

## 1. Valuation Authority

### 1.1 Clause IDs

- `INV-VAL-001`: Stock valuation shall be governed by a defined valuation method.
- `INV-VAL-002`: FIFO shall be mandatory unless a future governed configuration replaces it.
- `INV-VAL-003`: Cost layers shall remain auditable.

### 1.2 Valuation law

- `INV-VAL-010`: Stock value shall track actual movement cost layers.
- `INV-VAL-011`: Late receipts shall be capable of correcting prior negative-stock valuation effects.
- `INV-VAL-012`: Stock value tracking shall remain traceable to movement records.
