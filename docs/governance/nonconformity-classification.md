# Nonconformity Classification

## 1. Categories
1.1 Constitutional nonconformity.
1.2 Workflow nonconformity.
1.3 Structural nonconformity.
1.4 Financial nonconformity.
1.5 Compliance nonconformity.
1.6 Traceability nonconformity.
1.7 UX and route-truth nonconformity.
1.8 Data-integrity nonconformity.
1.9 Evidence nonconformity.

## 2. Severity Bands
2.1 Critical.
2.2 High.
2.3 Medium.
2.4 Low.
2.5 Informational.

## 3. Escalation Rules
3.1 Critical and high nonconformities shall require escalation.
3.2 Evidence nonconformity shall block closure until evidence is sufficient.
3.3 Constitutional nonconformity shall be treated as top priority.

## 4. Clause IDs
4.1 Stable clauses in this document use the prefix `AUD-CLS`.

## 5. Enforceable Clauses
- `AUD-CLS-001`: Nonconformity shall be classified by category.
- `AUD-CLS-002`: Severity shall be assigned by band.
- `AUD-CLS-003`: Critical and high findings shall escalate.
- `AUD-CLS-004`: Evidence nonconformity shall block closure.
