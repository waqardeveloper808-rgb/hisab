# Payroll Accounting Integration

## 1. Purpose
1.1 Payroll shall post into accounting through governed journal entries.
1.2 Payroll shall remain synchronized with accounting truth.

## 2. Required Accounts
2.1 Salary expense account shall be used for payroll expense.
2.2 Payroll payable account shall be used for liabilities.
2.3 Cash or bank impact shall be recognized when payment occurs.

## 3. Integration Law
3.1 Payroll shall generate journal entries.
3.2 Payroll shall not exist as a standalone financial event.
3.3 Payroll accounting output shall be traceable to payroll source records.

## 4. Clause IDs
4.1 Stable clauses in this document use the prefix `HR-ACC`.

## 5. Enforceable Clauses
- `HR-ACC-001`: Payroll shall generate journal entries.
- `HR-ACC-002`: Salary expense shall be recorded.
- `HR-ACC-003`: Payroll payable shall be recorded.
- `HR-ACC-004`: Cash or bank impact shall be recorded when paid.
- `HR-ACC-005`: Standalone payroll shall be forbidden.
