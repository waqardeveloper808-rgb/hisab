# Intelligence Layer Constitution

## 1. Purpose
1.1 The intelligence layer shall provide rule-based assistance only.
1.2 The intelligence layer shall not act as autonomous AI.
1.3 The intelligence layer shall assist with suggestions, validation awareness, and anomaly warnings.

## 2. Allowed Functions
2.1 Account suggestions shall be pattern-based.
2.2 Item suggestions shall be pattern-based.
2.3 Vendor and customer pattern recognition shall be rule-based.
2.4 Input guidance shall provide VAT hints, required field prompts, and formatting guidance.
2.5 Behavior memory shall rank frequent patterns.
2.6 Audit-aware intelligence shall warn or block critical anomalies before save.

## 3. Forbidden Functions
3.1 The intelligence layer shall not invent business truth.
3.2 The intelligence layer shall not override mandatory validation.
3.3 The intelligence layer shall not silently auto-correct critical financial data.

## 4. Clause IDs
4.1 Stable clauses in this document use the prefix `INTEL-CON`.
4.2 Future control points and audits shall reference the exact clause IDs defined here.

## 5. Enforceable Clauses
- `INTEL-CON-001`: Intelligence shall be rule-based only.
- `INTEL-CON-002`: Intelligence shall not act autonomously.
- `INTEL-CON-003`: Suggestions shall follow governed patterns.
- `INTEL-CON-004`: Critical anomalies shall be warned or blocked.
- `INTEL-CON-005`: Mandatory validation shall not be bypassed.
