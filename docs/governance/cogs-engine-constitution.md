# COGS Engine Constitution

Updated: 2026-04-23
Status: Governing cost-of-goods-sold generation law.

## 1. COGS Authority

### 1.1 Clause IDs

- `INV-COGS-001`: The COGS engine shall generate cost of goods sold automatically on sale where stock-linked sale logic applies.
- `INV-COGS-002`: COGS shall map to accounting truth.
- `INV-COGS-003`: COGS shall extract cost from the valuation layer.

### 1.2 COGS law

- `INV-COGS-010`: Sale-related stock-out shall create governed COGS impact.
- `INV-COGS-011`: COGS mismatch shall be treated as an inventory and accounting defect.
- `INV-COGS-012`: COGS impact shall be visible in profit reporting and ledger mapping.
